# Sunrise Caravans — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: [DATE]. Plain-English template tailored to Sunrise Caravans. Have your
accountant or a lawyer give it a quick once-over before publishing — mainly to confirm
the Privacy Act applies to you (it almost certainly does at caravan sale values) and that
the overseas-tools section matches what you actually use.*

---

Sunrise Caravans ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what
personal information we collect, why, how we keep it safe, and the choices you have. We
handle personal information in line with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian
Privacy Principles (APPs).

## 1. Who we are
Sunrise Caravans — 290 Eastern Service Road, Burpengary QLD 4505.
Phone (07) 3888 4455 · Email steve@sunrisecaravans.com.au · sunrisecaravans.com.au

## 2. What we collect
Only what we need to help you with a caravan:
- **Contact details** — name, email, phone, postal/home address.
- **Enquiry details** — the van you're interested in, your budget, tow vehicle, what you're after.
- **Sale & service details** — purchase, delivery, trade-in valuations, service and warranty history.
- **Finance details** — only if you ask us to help arrange finance (handled with your consent).
We do **not** collect sensitive information (health, etc.) and we don't ask for it.

## 3. How we collect it
Directly from you — when you enquire through our website, call us, visit the yard, meet us
at a caravan show, or buy or service a van. If someone refers you, we may record that too.

## 4. Why we use it
- To answer your enquiry and help you find the right van.
- To prepare quotes, arrange delivery, trade-ins, finance, service and warranty.
- To stay in touch about your van and, **only with your consent**, send you news and offers.
- To meet our legal and warranty obligations.

## 5. How we store and protect it
Your information is held in a secure, access-controlled customer system hosted in
**Australia (Sydney)**. It is **encrypted**, both stored and in transit. Only our authorised
team can access it, each with their own login and only the access their role needs, and every
change, deletion and export is recorded. We never sell your information.

## 6. Who we share it with
- **Finance** — with your consent, our finance partner (Finetic) to arrange your loan.
- **Service providers** — only those who help us run the business (and only what they need).
- **Email tools** — if you opt in to marketing, your name and email may be processed through
  our email provider. *[If you use Mailchimp, note: Mailchimp stores data in the United States,
  so by opting in you consent to that overseas handling.]*
- **When required by law.**
We never sell or rent your information to anyone.

## 7. Marketing & your choices
We only send marketing if you've opted in. Every marketing email has an unsubscribe link, and
you can opt out any time by clicking it or emailing us. We honour opt-outs promptly.

## 8. Accessing or correcting your information
You can ask us what we hold about you, correct it, or ask us to delete it. Just email
steve@sunrisecaravans.com.au or call (07) 3888 4455. We'll respond within a reasonable time
(usually within 30 days) at no charge.

## 9. How long we keep it
We keep your information while you're a customer or active enquiry, and for as long as the law
or warranty requires. After that we securely delete it.

## 10. Complaints
If you're unhappy with how we've handled your information, contact Steve on the details above
and we'll work to fix it. If you're still not satisfied, you can contact the Office of the
Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or 1300 363 992.

## 11. Changes
We may update this policy from time to time. The current version is always on our website with
the date it was last updated.
